Welcome to our lovely's bakery! We are a family-owned and operated business, passionate about

creating delicious baked goods using only the freshest and highest quality ingredients.

Our bakery first opened its doors in 2019, and we have been delighting customers with our

freshly baked breads, pastries, and other treats ever since. Our pastry chef, Roechan, has over

4.5 years of experience in the baking industry and is always coming up with new and

creative recipes to showcase in our store.

In addition to our regular menu items, we also offer custom cakes and desserts for special

occasions such as weddings, birthdays, and corporate events. No matter the occasion, we

thrive to create the perfect dessert to make your event extra special.
